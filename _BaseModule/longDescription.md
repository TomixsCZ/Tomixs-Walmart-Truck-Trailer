# Walmart Trailer Truck
Hello. This mod adds a prop of a Walmart Truck Trailer

## Functions
- Changeable Colors(White, Gray, Black)
- Apply a font

##  Fonts
Font is **included**

### How to install the font
1. Click on the W Icon on the Top-Left
2. Go to City Fonts 
3. Press **+** and click on the Puzzle-Piece.  
4. Choose "Tomixs Walmart Truck Trailer"
6. Select the font
7. Look at the Screenshot Font Tutorial on how to change them for Dynamic Signs
